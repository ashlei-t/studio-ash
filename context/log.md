# therecord.md
> field notes — the daily record
> one entry per day, a paragraph not a list
> morning energy implicit in what you write, not labeled
> captures tagged throughout the day, evening closes the loop
> at end of season: the raw material for insight gathering

---

## how a good entry reads
Not a to-do list. Not a status report.
A paragraph you could read in six months and remember what the day actually felt like.
What was present in the morning. What moved during the day. What you noticed.
What you let go of. What you're carrying into tomorrow.
